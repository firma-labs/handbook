# Introduction

> Through divine inspiration and grit, our family at Firma Labs is flipping the world upside down. We aren't just building tech — we are building the future.

This is the handbook for Firma Labs — the people, the work, and the way we go about it. It describes what we're building, how we treat each other, what we expect of ourselves, and what you can expect of us.

## What Firma Labs is

Firma Labs builds the compute chain, the economic models, and the edge hardware that let real communities pursue true freedom. Charter cities, Special Economic Zones, sovereign communities — they use what we make. We're also building our own network state alongside them.

Three divisions do this work:

* **firma labs** — the chain, the economic models, the agentic layer, the network state simulator. The substrate other communities deploy on.
* **firma edge** — modular pods, nodes, compute, mesh, and secure edge infrastructure. The devices people touch, sleep in, and run a community on.
* **firma energy** — solar, batteries, and distributed grid infrastructure. Energy generated at the edge, owned by the people using it.

Our capital partners fund the work. The Firma Foundation deploys it into the communities we serve.
