# Chapter 9 — Getting Started

Your first weeks.

Onboarding at Firma Labs is designed to orient you to the vision, plug you into the agentic orchestration, and get you shipping within your first week.

---

## Week 1 — Orient

You read the handbook. You meet your Partner and your closest collaborators. You set up your tools: Notion, ClickUp, Slack, the agentic harness. You get your Starlink. You get your first lunch in the office — or your first grocery stipend if you're remote. You make your first commit.

## Month 1 — Ship

You ship something real within your first thirty days. Not a tutorial project. Real work that ends up in production or in front of a customer. Your Partner makes sure you have a first project that is shippable, meaningful, and within your reach. You move. We watch what you do, learn how you think, and calibrate together.

## Day 90 — Review, and the Trust opens

At 90 days we do your first review together. It is not a grading exercise. It is a conversation about trajectory — where you are, where you're headed, what support you need, what you want to build next. At that point, your Trust opens. The monthly yield begins.

## Year 1 — Your first deployment

Every Partner and Associate goes on at least one deployment per year. Within your first twelve months, you will have been in the field with a real community — hands dirty, family with you, laying rails. This is not optional. It is what makes a Firma Labs builder different from every other engineer or designer or PM in the world.
