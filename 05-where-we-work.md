# Chapter 5 — Where We Work

Five anchors. And wherever else the work takes you.

Firma Labs is distributed by design, but there are specific places the work concentrates. Each has a character, a function, and a welcome mat.

---

## Dallas HQ — Texas, US

The operational center. Executive work, core engineering, finance, and people operations. Where the org makes its biggest decisions in the same room.

## The Ranch — outside Dallas

Regenerative farming tech, hardware prototyping, and the place where land-based sovereignty starts looking real. Builders come here to make things with their hands.

## Philippines offices — APAC

Global events and field operations. When we ship a booth to Devcon or land a team at an ETH event, it's run from here.

## Panama — LATAM

Remote-friendly, crypto-friendly, welcoming to network states and digital nomads exploring a new way of life. A hub for team members who work best on the move.

## Puerto Rico retreat — yearly gathering

Once a year, the whole Firma Labs family gathers in Puerto Rico. Partners. Associates. Their families. It is not a conference — it is a reunion. (Did we mention you can also just work from there any time?)

---

## The deployment requirement

Every Partner and Associate at Firma Labs goes on at least one deployment per year. Hands dirty. Serve and love others. Remote places encouraged.

You could be deployed to Patagonia, Namibia, Kazakhstan, or wherever the communities we serve happen to be. Adventure and care for others should be embedded in your core. If the thought of a week in a remote settlement feels like a punishment rather than a privilege, Firma Labs is not the right home.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Family-covered deployment.** Deployments are paid — **including your family's travel and accommodations.** Because if we ask you to go to the edge of the world to serve a community, we are not asking you to leave your people behind to do it.
{% endhint %}
