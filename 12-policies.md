# Chapter 12 — Policies

The policies that exist.

We keep this section short on purpose. Policies exist where they are needed, and nowhere else. Everything here is real. Nothing here is ceremonial.

---

| | |
|---|---|
| **Equal opportunity** | Firma Labs is an equal opportunity employer. We hire, promote, and compensate without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, age, or veteran status. We hire on character and capacity. |
| **Conduct** | Harassment, bullying, retaliation, and dishonesty are incompatible with the culture we've described. If any of it appears, we address it directly and quickly. The values in Chapter 2 are also the conduct standard. |
| **Privacy** | Your personal information is handled with the same care we demand of the systems we build for others. We don't sell data, share it beyond what's operationally required, or monitor activity beyond what's needed for security and safety. |
| **Complaints** | If something feels wrong, raise it. Your Partner first. {{PEOPLE_OPS_LEAD}} in People Operations second. An independent board advisor third if needed. We don't retaliate. We repair. |
| **IT & security hygiene** | Use 2FA everywhere. Use a password manager. Don't share credentials. Don't click suspicious links. Basic hygiene, applied rigorously. Security is downstream of small habits. |
| **At-will employment** | US team members are at-will employees. Either side can end the relationship at any time, for any reason. We think this is what trust looks like when written as law — not a threat, but an honest statement that we're choosing each other every day. |

---

If a situation arises that isn't covered here, we fall back on the values, the ethos, and a conversation with your Partner. That's almost always enough. When it isn't, we write a new policy, and we add it here.

---

*End of handbook.*
