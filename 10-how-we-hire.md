# Chapter 10 — How We Hire

What we look for.

The hiring bar at Firma Labs is narrow on purpose. We are not trying to grow headcount; we are trying to find the specific people who make this work possible.

---

## The hard criteria

| | |
|---|---|
| **Powerful** | You can move things. You don't wait for permission. You ship. If nobody gave you direction for a month, you would still be doing the right work. |
| **Moral framework** | You have one, whatever it is, and it's load-bearing. We don't have to manage your whereabouts because we already know what you'd do when no one is looking. |
| **Purpose over career arc** | You are compelled by what we're building. You are not optimizing for the next title, the next company, or the next exit. |
| **Willing to deploy** | Patagonia, Namibia, Kazakhstan — wherever the communities we serve are. The thought of going excites you rather than drains you. |
| **Agentic-fluent** | You don't treat AI like a typo checker. You already use agentic leverage in your work, or you are hungry to learn how. |
| **Radical faith** | You believe things that look impossible are often just things not yet attempted by the right team. That belief is not naïve for you — it is operational. |

---

{% hint style="warning" %}
**What we don't hire for.** We don't hire for resumes. We don't hire for pedigree. We don't hire for culture fit as it's usually meant. We hire for **character and capacity**, and we interview to understand both — not to check boxes.
{% endhint %}
