# Chapter 11 — How We Leave

When a season ends.

Most companies treat departure as a failure mode. We treat it as the natural completion of a season. Written once, clearly, at the end.

---

## What stays with you

Your **economic rights** vest as agreed. The portion that has vested by your departure is yours, represented on-chain as Fragmints, paying distributions from the revenue of what you helped build under the terms described in your individual agreement. Economic rights are designed to be an inheritance, not a paycheck. Leaving does not end the rights you've already earned.

The remaining mechanics — vesting schedules, distribution timing, what happens to unvested portions, beneficiary arrangements — are defined in your individual agreement with Firma Labs.

Your **relationship with Firma Labs** does not end. Former team members are family. Come back later. Advise a project. Join another deployment. The door stays open because the work we did together mattered.

---

## What we ask of you

Leave well. Hand off with honor. Tell the truth about why you're going. Give us the feedback you wish someone had given the company six months earlier. Close the loops you opened. Bless the people coming up behind you.

That's it. That's the agreement. Come as family. Leave as family. Always.
